Overview

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data from Side Quest: Routine Breaker at any time. Please read through the information below before submitting your request.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed within 30 days:

  • Your Google account link (name, email, profile picture)
  • Level, XP, and progression data
  • Streak history and dice count
  • Quest completion logs
  • All Firestore records associated with your user ID
⚠️ Locally cached data stored on your device (via Hive) will be removed when you uninstall the app. We recommend uninstalling the app after your deletion request is processed.

How to Request Deletion

1

Send an email to nautolab.dev@gmail.com with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request – Side Quest"

2

Include the email address associated with your Google account used to sign in to the app.

3

We will confirm your request and process the deletion within 30 days.

4

You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

📧 Send Deletion Request

Data Retention After Deletion

We do not retain any personal data after deletion is processed. Anonymous, aggregated analytics data (if any) that cannot be linked back to you may be retained for service improvement purposes.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Any XP, levels, streaks, or progress will be lost forever.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the deletion process, contact us at nautolab.dev@gmail.com or visit our Privacy Policy.
