Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how nautolab ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Side Quest: Routine Breaker mobile application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Effective date: March 2026

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve the App:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture via Firebase Authentication.
  • App Usage Data: Your level, XP, streak count, dice count, quest completion history, and last login date — stored in Firebase Firestore.
  • Device Identifiers: Advertising ID collected by Google AdMob for serving personalized ads.
  • Local Cache: App data may be cached locally on your device using Hive for offline functionality.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and sync your progress across devices
  • To assign and track your daily quests, XP, level, and streak
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To provide Rewarded Video ads in exchange for additional reroll dice
  • To improve app performance and user experience

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Firebase Firestore — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob — policies.google.com/privacy
We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email below and we will process your request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS) and Firebase's built-in security rules to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
